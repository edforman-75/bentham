Problem Analysis: Transportation (52%)

Root Cause: Jurisdictional confusion. Roads are managed by ACHD (Ada County Highway District), not the City of Boise. Users searching for "Boise transportation" don't find clear answers because:
  • No central hub page explaining transportation in Boise
  • ACHD content isn't clearly linked from city site
  • Valley Regional Transit (public transit) is a separate entity
  • Airport is managed by Boise Airport Authority

Failing Queries

  • "Boise public transportation options"
  • "How do I get around Boise without a car?"
  • "Who maintains roads in Boise?"
  • "Report a pothole in Boise" (actually ACHD responsibility)

Problem Analysis: Leadership (82%)

Root Cause: Mayor and city council information exists but lacks structured data that helps AI platforms understand and surface it.

Opportunities

  • Add Person schema to mayor's bio page
  • Create individual council member pages with structured data
  • Add Event schema for council meetings

Context: Google AI Overviews

Google AI Overviews appear for only 15% of queries. However, testing the same queries for Denver, Austin, and Seattle shows this is a Google platform behavior, not a Boise issue.

Methodology: Panopticon Observation

What is Panopticon?

Panopticon is named after Jeremy Bentham's 1791 design for an observation structure—a place where an observer could watch without being seen. In the same way, Panopticon observes AI systems in their natural state, querying surfaces without revealing that systematic research is underway.

This approach captures authentic AI responses—the same answers that real citizens, businesses, and visitors would receive when searching for information.

Scoring Methodology

Answer Quality

For each query, we measured whether the response contained relevant information about Boise. A response "mentions Boise" if it includes specific, useful information answering the user's question about Boise.

Weighted Score

The overall Answer Quality Score weights each surface by its estimated share of real-world queries:

Surface Weight Rationale
Google Search 75% Dominant search engine
ChatGPT 12% Leading conversational AI
Bing Search 8% Second-largest search engine
Meta AI 3% Emerging platform with large reach
Other 2% Perplexity, Claude, etc.

Formula: Weighted Score = (Google × 0.75) + (ChatGPT × 0.12) + (Bing × 0.08) + (Meta × 0.03) + (Est. Other × 0.02)

Limitations

  • Point-in-time snapshot: AI responses vary over time as models update. This study reflects January 2026.
  • Location: Queries were executed from US IP addresses. Results may vary by geography.
  • AI Overview variability: Google AI Overviews appear dynamically based on query, user, and context. Our detection may not capture all instances.
  • Platform weights are estimates: Actual query distribution varies by demographic and use case.
