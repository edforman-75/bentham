Prioritize: Key Issues & Opportunities

Critical gaps that affect how AI systems represent Boise to residents and visitors.

Critical ACHD vs City Confusion

AI systems frequently tell residents to contact the City for road issues (potholes, signals, street repairs) when ACHD is actually responsible. This causes misdirected calls and resident frustration.

Critical Leadership Information Outdated

Questions about City Council members, department heads, and leadership often return outdated or incorrect names. No single authoritative source exists on cityofboise.org.

Critical Service Information Scattered

"How do I pay my utility bill?" and similar questions get incomplete answers because information is spread across multiple pages without structured data.

Important Event Information Missing

Questions about Boise events (Spirit of Boise, festivals, concerts) often return outdated dates or no information because events lack Schema.org markup.

Important City Facts Inconsistent

Basic questions (population, elevation, founding date) get varying answers because this information isn't consolidated on an official city page.

Opportunity Government Structure Unclear

Questions about Boise's form of government, council districts, and election dates could be answered more accurately with better structured content.

Act: Recommended Actions

Step-by-step Umbraco instructions for each improvement. All changes can be made by content editors.

High Priority Add GovernmentOrganization Schema to Homepage

Problem: AI systems inconsistently report basic city information like the mayor's name, address, and main phone number.

Solution

  • Add Schema.org GovernmentOrganization markup to homepage
  • Include official name, address, phone, mayor, and founding date
  • AI systems prioritize this authoritative structured data
Umbraco Instructions
  1. Log into Umbraco at cityofboise.org/umbraco
  2. Navigate: Content → Home
  3. Click the SEO tab
  4. Paste this JSON-LD into Schema Markup field:
{ "@context": "https://schema.org", "@type": "GovernmentOrganization", "name": "City of Boise", "url": "https://www.cityofboise.org", "description": "Official government of the City of Boise, Idaho", "address": { "@type": "PostalAddress", "streetAddress": "150 N Capitol Blvd", "addressLocality": "Boise", "addressRegion": "ID", "postalCode": "83702" }, "telephone": "+1-208-384-4422", "foundingDate": "1863", "member": { "@type": "Person", "name": "Lauren McLean", "jobTitle": "Mayor" } }
  1. Click Save and Publish
Expected Impact: Immediate improvement for "Who is the mayor?" and basic city contact queries.

High Priority Create Service FAQ Pages with Schema

Problem: "How do I pay my utility bill?" gets incomplete answers because information is scattered.

Solution

  • Create dedicated FAQ pages for major services (Utilities, Permits, Trash)
  • Add Schema.org FAQPage markup
  • Use actual citizen questions as headers
Umbraco Instructions
  1. Navigate: Content → Services → Utilities
  2. Click the SEO tab
  3. Add FAQPage schema:
{ "@context": "https://schema.org", "@type": "FAQPage", "mainEntity": [{ "@type": "Question", "name": "How do I pay my utility bill in Boise?", "acceptedAnswer": { "@type": "Answer", "text": "Pay online at cityofboise.org/utilities, by phone at 208-384-3900, by mail, or in person at City Hall." } }] }
Expected Impact: AI systems will quote FAQ answers directly. +10-15% improvement in service queries.

High Priority Create ACHD Clarification Page

Problem: AI systems tell residents to contact City of Boise for roads, but ACHD handles all roads.

Solution

  • Create a "Roads & Streets" page explaining ACHD responsibility
  • Include ACHD contact info and common scenarios
  • Clarify what the City vs ACHD handles
Suggested Content
ROAD & STREET SERVICES The Ada County Highway District (ACHD) maintains all public roads in Ada County, including Boise. CONTACT ACHD FOR: • Potholes: achdidaho.org or 208-387-6100 • Traffic signals • Street repairs • Road construction CITY OF BOISE HANDLES: • Sidewalk repairs (city property) • Street lighting (contact Idaho Power) • Traffic enforcement (Boise Police)
Expected Impact: Eliminates misdirected calls. AI will correctly direct road inquiries to ACHD.

Medium Priority Update Leadership Page with Schema

Problem: Questions about City Council and department heads return outdated information.

Solution

  • Create/update comprehensive Leadership page
  • Add Person schema for each official
  • Keep updated when leadership changes
Leadership Schema
{ "@context": "https://schema.org", "@type": "ItemList", "name": "City of Boise Leadership", "itemListElement": [{ "@type": "Person", "name": "Lauren McLean", "jobTitle": "Mayor", "worksFor": {"@type": "GovernmentOrganization", "name": "City of Boise"} }] }
