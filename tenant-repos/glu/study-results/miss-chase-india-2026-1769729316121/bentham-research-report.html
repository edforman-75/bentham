Issues Found

Critical: Product Descriptions Missing
Many product pages have empty or minimal descriptions. AI systems cannot recommend products they don't have information about.
Warning: Incomplete JSON-LD Schema
Product schema exists but key fields are empty (description, brand consistency, reviews).
Warning: Brand Inconsistency

Multiple pages with "#N/A" names and $0 prices are being indexed - should be noindexed.

Strategic Recommendations

Priority Actions to Close the AI Visibility Gap

1
Fix Product Data Quality

Add comprehensive descriptions to ALL products. Include: fabric details, fit guide, styling suggestions, care instructions. AI systems need this content to make recommendations.
Impact: Foundation for all other improvements + reduces returns by setting accurate expectations

2
Content Strategy for Category Queries

Create authoritative content targeting "best [category] brands India" queries. Publish guides, comparisons, and expert content that AI systems will cite.
Impact: Directly addresses the category query invisibility problem

3
Build External Citations

Get featured in "best brands" listicles, fashion roundups, and comparison articles on high-authority sites. These are the sources AI systems cite.
Impact: AI Overviews pull from these external sources

4
Implement AI-Ready Schema Markup

Complete JSON-LD Product schema with all fields. Add FAQ schema, Review schema, and Organization schema to provide structured data AI systems prefer.
Impact: Improves AI's ability to parse and present product info

5
Brand Consistency Cleanup

Standardize brand naming across all touchpoints. Decide: Is it "Miss Chase" or "Chase Haul"? Update site, products, social, and marketplace listings to match.
Impact: Reduces AI confusion about brand identity

6
PR & Editorial Coverage

Secure coverage in fashion publications, startup press, and industry reports. Target articles about "D2C brands", "Indian fashion startups", and "women's fashion".
Impact: Builds authority signals AI systems use for ranking
