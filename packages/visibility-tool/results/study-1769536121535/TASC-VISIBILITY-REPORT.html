Recommendations

1

Close the "Best Activewear" Content Gap

TASC is invisible in generic "best workout clothes" and "best activewear" searches. Create comprehensive content targeting these high-volume, high-intent queries. Lululemon and Vuori currently dominate this space.

2

Strengthen Sustainability Narrative

Despite bamboo/eco-friendly positioning, TASC is not appearing in sustainability-focused AI recommendations. Consider more prominent certifications, partnerships with eco-focused publications, and enhanced sustainability content on tascperformance.com.

3

Double Down on Bamboo Differentiation

TASC owns "bamboo athletic wear" in AI responses (100% mention rate in bamboo queries). Expand bamboo-specific content to maintain and strengthen this competitive advantage.

4

Optimize Competitor Comparison Content

When competitors are listed first in queries, TASC mention rate drops. Create comparison content positioning TASC first (e.g., "TASC vs Lululemon" rather than "Lululemon vs TASC").

5

Expand Third-Party Citation Presence

While tascperformance.com is the top cited source, expanding presence on high-authority review sites (GearJunkie, Men's Health, OutdoorGearLab) would improve visibility in recommendation queries where third-party validation matters.
